Executive Summary

Greenfield Industrial Services generates approximately 180 tons/month of mixed Construction & Demolition waste from active renovation sites in the greater Phoenix area. The stream is currently handled by a regional hauler with no formal sorting at source. Audit documentation confirms material composition at a high level, but two contamination incidents in Q1 2026 suggest concrete fines and rebar fragments are entering the load mix inconsistently.

Blocker: Compliance certification (EPA 261.4(b)(1)) expired Feb 2026 — no new handler will accept without renewal. Estimated 3–4 week delay if not escalated.
