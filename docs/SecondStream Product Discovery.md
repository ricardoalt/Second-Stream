# **SecondStream Product Discovery Report** 

---

# **1\. User Context**

## **Primary User Profile**

**User Type:** Industrial Byproduct Broker / Secondary Materials Trader  
**Role:** Senior Operator / Business Developer  
**Environment:**

* Highly regulated (EPA / RCRA / transport laws / international shipping)  
* Cross-border logistics  
* Multi-party coordination (facility → intermediaries → transporters → processors → end users)  
* Fragmented information sources  
* Heavy reliance on tacit knowledge

## **Goals**

* Turn “waste” into recoverable value  
* Reduce client disposal costs  
* Maintain regulatory compliance  
* Avoid operational disruptions  
* Deliver simple proposals from complex inputs  
* Scale without hiring 10 more operations people

## **Constraints**

* Incomplete or incorrect client data  
* Regulatory complexity  
* Intermediaries block information  
* Legal/NDAs slow discovery  
* Logistics unpredictability  
* Small team, no large ops department  
* Intellectual property not systematized

# **4\. Current Workflow (Step-by-Step)**

1. Lead arrives (often via intermediary)  
2. Initial material description (often vague)  
3. Ask for:  
   * SDS or waste profile  
   * Manifest  
   * Lab analysis (if available)  
4. Ask about:  
   * Process that generated material  
   * Volume and recurrence  
   * Location  
   * Current cost  
   * Timeline  
5. Determine regulatory classification  
6. Decide whether lab analysis required  
7. Call known buyers / industry contacts  
8. Explore potential use pathways  
9. Negotiate logistics  
10. Price proposal manually  
11. Manage transport coordination  
12. Track compliance manually

Highly manual. Knowledge resides in people’s heads.

# **9\. MVP Definition**

### **Goal:**

Solve discovery bottleneck.

## **MVP Features**

1. Structured Intake Wizard  
2. Process-Origin Capture  
3. Volume \+ Recurrence Capture  
4. Cost Breakdown Capture  
5. Basic Regulatory Reference Engine  
6. Historical Pricing Lookup  
7. Deal Stage Tracker (blocker visibility)

### **Rationale**

If discovery becomes structured:

* Pipeline velocity increases  
* Knowledge becomes institutional  
* Pricing improves  
* Scale becomes possible

MVP does NOT include:

* Full logistics automation  
* Vendor graph tracking  
* Predictive opportunity engine

Those are Phase 2+.
